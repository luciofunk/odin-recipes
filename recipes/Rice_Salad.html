Rice Salad

Rice Salad description

The Rice Salad born in Italy, we don't know in which part of Italy the recipe was born, but we can say with certainty that it was a very popular fresh meal for those who went to the beach

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • hard-boiled eggs
  • Tuna
  • wurstel
  • Cut Tomato
  • Ingredient of your choice

Preparation

  1. Boil the rice and drain it
  2. Let the rice cool and add all cutted listed Ingredient
  3. Put in fridge for a couple of hours
