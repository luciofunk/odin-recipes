Lasagna

Lasagna description

The lasagna born in Emilia-Romagna, a region in Italy.
It's one of most famous dishes in world. Baked lasagna consists of a sheet of egg pasta cut into roughly rectangular sheets called lasagne,
which, once boiled and drained, are arranged in a sequence of layers, separated by a filling that varies in relation to the different traditions locals.

Ingredients

  • oil
  • one onion
  • Lasasgne(pasta layer)
  • Tomato puree
  • Minced beef 500 gr.
  • Besciamella
  • Parmiggiano reggiano(cheese)

Preparation

  1. Put oil and a chopped onion in a pan and leave to fry
  2. When the chopped onion is golden add the minced beef and add some salt
  3. When the the beef is cooked add the Tomato puree
  4. Leave to cook for a couple of hours
  5. take the pasta layers and boil it for ten minutes
  6. Add the first layer of lasagna to the baking tray
  7. Add the sauce of tomato and beef and Besciamella e cover with a layer of pasta
  8. repeat the operation till to fill the baking tray
  9. On the top some tomato and a sprinkling of cheese
  10. Place in the electric oven preheated to 180 degrees for approximately an hour and a half
